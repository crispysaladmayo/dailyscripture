Chicken & carrot rice bowl

Total time ~35 min · Active ~15 min

Per serving (estimate): about 320 calories, 18g protein, 30g carbs, and 12g fat—rough numbers for planning, not a label.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice, 2 cups
  • Chicken thigh, diced
  • Carrot, sliced
  • Garlic, oil, salt

Steps

  1. Sauté chicken until cooked through.
  2. Add carrot and cook until tender.
  3. Fold in rice; season to taste.
